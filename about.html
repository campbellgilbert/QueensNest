Queens Nest Homestay: About

About The Homestay

Queens Nest Homestay is a beautiful and serene homestay located in the quaint village of Rungli Rangliot, which is situated in the Darjeeling district of West Bengal. This homestay is located on a hilltop, little above the road, approximately 60 steps away from the main road, offering a peaceful and tranquil environment to its guests.

Accommodation

The Queens Nest Homestay offers comfortable accommodation options for its guests. The rooms are well-furnished and equipped with modern amenities to make your stay comfortable and memorable. The homestay offers double occupancy rooms, family rooms and a dormitory room, which is ideal for groups.

The homestay also has a beautiful outside sitting area where guests can relax and enjoy the scenic beauty of the surrounding the Himalayan mountain range.

Tea Garden

Rungli Rangliot is famous for its tea gardens, and guests staying at Queens Nest Homestay can take a walk to the tea gardens and experience the beauty of nature. The lush green tea gardens spread over acres of land is a sight to behold. Guests can also enjoy a cup of tea, made from freshly plucked tea leaves, which is available at the homestay.

Sightseeing and Tourist Activities

Queens Nest Homestay is an ideal base for exploring the scenic beauty and tourist attractions of Darjeeling. Guests can visit the nearby tourist spots, such as the Takdha orchid garden,lamahatta park,Tinchulay view point, Gumba dara view point, teesta view point, Teesta Vally durbin dara, 25km away Tiger Hill, Batasia Loop, Ghoom Monastery, and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.

One can also indulge in adventurous activities like trekking, camping, and river rafting. The nearby hiking trails offer spectacular views of the Himalayan mountain range, valleys, and waterfalls.

The homestay also offers guided tours to nearby places of interest, and guests can explore the local culture, food, and lifestyle of the villagers.

Food

The Queens Nest Homestay offers delicious home-cooked meals to its guests, which include local delicacies The food is prepared using fresh and organic ingredients sourced from nearby villages and farms.

Getting There

Queens Nest Homestay is located approximately 38 km from the nearest airport, Bagdogra, and 35km from the nearest railway station, New Jalpaiguri. Guests can hire a taxi or take a shared jeep from Siliguri or Darjeeling to reach the homestay.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Queens Nest Homestay is from September to December and from March to May. During this time, the weather is pleasant, and the views of the Himalayan mountain range are spectacular.

In conclusion, Queens Nest Homestay is an ideal place to stay for nature lovers and those looking for a peaceful and relaxing holiday. With its beautiful location, comfortable accommodation, delicious food, and tourist activities, this homestay is a must-visit for those planning a trip to Darjeeling.
